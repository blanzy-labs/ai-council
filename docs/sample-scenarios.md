# Sample Scenarios

Use these scenarios to test AI Council v0.1.0 with the mock provider or OpenAI.

## Startup Idea Review

- Suggested title: Local-first startup review
- Topic: Should we build a local-first app for founders to pressure-test startup ideas?
- Recommended mode: `ask_council`
- Recommended personas: `moderator`, `strategist`, `skeptic`, `builder`, `customer_advocate`
- Suggested follow-up question: What is the riskiest assumption we should test first?

## Product MVP Planning

- Suggested title: MVP planning council
- Topic: Define the smallest useful MVP for a multi-persona council product.
- Recommended mode: `council_discussion`
- Recommended personas: `moderator`, `strategist`, `builder`, `skeptic`
- Suggested follow-up question: Builder, what should be cut from the first version?

## Book/Theme Discussion

- Suggested title: Theme discussion
- Topic: Discuss the theme of ambition versus responsibility in a novel or essay.
- Recommended mode: `ask_council`
- Recommended personas: `moderator`, `ethicist`, `contrarian`, `skeptic`
- Suggested follow-up question: What is the strongest counter-reading of this theme?

## Security/Risk Critique

- Suggested title: Risk critique
- Topic: Review the risks of releasing a local AI demo app to early testers.
- Recommended mode: `ask_council`
- Recommended personas: `moderator`, `skeptic`, `builder`, `ethicist`
- Suggested follow-up question: What risk is easiest to mitigate before release?

## Personal Decision Council

- Suggested title: Decision council
- Topic: Should I focus the next month on product polish, content, or customer discovery?
- Recommended mode: `ask_one`
- Recommended personas: `strategist`, `skeptic`, `customer_advocate`
- Suggested follow-up question: What would make this decision clearer within one week?
